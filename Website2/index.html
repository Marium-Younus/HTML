Maruti

Maruti Suzuki India Ltd. is a leading manufacturer of four-wheeler in India. Born in 1983 with the mission to motorise India, Maruti was a joint venture between Government of India and Suzuki Motor Corporation , Japan. It quickly grew into the largest compact car making company of India and remained so till 2004. The company started with Suzuki holding the minor stakes of the company while GoI holding the major stakes. As of present, GoI has disinvested its stakes in the company completely, and handed over the management of company to Suzuki Motor Corporation. Today, Maruti and its partners employ more than 75000 employees.
